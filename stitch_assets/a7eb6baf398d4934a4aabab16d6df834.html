Incorrect Question 4 of 10

In the JavaScript Event Loop, which of these has the highest priority during execution?

Your Answer
setTimeout Callback (Task Queue)
Correct Answer
Promise.then Callback (Microtask Queue)
psychology
AI Insight

"Common misconception: while `setTimeout` feels faster, the engine prioritizes the **Microtask Queue** (Promises, queueMicrotask) immediately after the current task finishes and before the next **Macrotask** begins. Macrotasks like `setTimeout` must wait for all pending microtasks to clear."

Incorrect Question 7 of 10

What happens to the remaining code in an `async` function after an `await` expression is encountered?

Your Answer
The main thread freezes until resolved
Correct Answer
It is scheduled as a microtask for future execution
psychology
AI Insight

"Syntactic sugar alert: `await` doesn't block. It essentially wraps the rest of your function in a `.then()` callback, allowing the main execution thread to continue with other tasks outside the async function."
